Toppings!


conduments

Ketchup, a beloved condiment, adds a tangy sweetness to a variety of dishes, from burgers to fries. Its vibrant red hue and versatile flavor make it a staple in many kitchens around the world.

Mustard, with its distinctive tangy flavor, is a versatile condiment that adds a bold kick to sandwiches, dressings, and marinades. Whether it's the classic yellow variety or the more robust Dijon, mustard's pungent taste elevates dishes with its unique zing.

Coleslaw, a refreshing side dish, combines shredded cabbage, carrots, and a creamy dressing for a crunchy and tangy flavor profile. Its versatility makes it a popular accompaniment to everything from barbecue to fried chicken, adding a refreshing contrast to savory mains.

vegetables

Onions, with their pungent aroma and versatile flavor, serve as a fundamental ingredient in countless culinary creations worldwide. Whether caramelized for sweetness, diced for texture, or enjoyed raw for a sharp bite, onions infuse dishes with their distinct essence, enriching soups, salads, and savory dishes alike.

Lettuce, with its crisp texture and mild flavor, forms the crunchy foundation of countless salads and sandwiches. Whether it's the delicate leaves of butter lettuce or the robust crunch of romaine, lettuce adds freshness and vibrancy to dishes, making it a staple in kitchens and restaurants worldwide.

Tomatoes, with their juicy flesh and vibrant color, are a versatile ingredient enjoyed in salads, sauces, sandwiches, and more. Whether sliced, diced, or pureed, tomatoes lend a burst of acidity and sweetness to dishes, elevating their flavor profile with a refreshing zest.

Pickles, brined and fermented cucumbers, offer a delightful crunch and tangy flavor to sandwiches, burgers, and salads. Whether enjoyed whole, sliced, or speared, pickles provide a refreshing contrast to savory dishes, adding a burst of acidity and complexity to each bite.


others

Eggs, a versatile culinary ingredient, can be prepared in numerous ways, from scrambled and fried to boiled and poached, offering endless possibilities for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. With their rich protein content and creamy texture, eggs serve as a staple in various cuisines worldwide, adding both flavor and nutritional value to a wide array of dishes.

Bacon, beloved for its crispy texture and savory flavor, is a staple ingredient in breakfasts, sandwiches, and salads around the world. Whether sizzling in a skillet or baked to perfection in the oven, bacon adds a satisfyingly smoky richness to dishes, making it a perennial favorite among food enthusiasts.

Crispy chicken, with its golden-brown exterior and tender interior, is a classic comfort food enjoyed in various forms, from fried chicken to chicken tenders. Whether coated in breadcrumbs or batter, crispy chicken delights the palate with its satisfying crunch and succulent meat, making it a perennial favorite for meals and snacks alike.

Crispy fish, with its golden-brown crust and flaky interior, offers a delightful texture and flavor experience that satisfies seafood lovers' cravings. Whether coated in breadcrumbs, batter, or cornmeal, crispy fish dishes bring a delicious crunch to the table, making them a popular choice for seafood enthusiasts everywhere.
